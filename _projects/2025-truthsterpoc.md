TRUTHSTER POC ("Implementazione della piattaforma TRUTHSTER per certificazione di media digitali mediante blockchain") aims to bring the TRUTHSTER platform to a market-ready service for certifying digital media content through blockchain. The project develops a mobile application, connected to a blockchain platform, that produces a «proof of validity» based on an innovative form of interaction between people: it authenticates the authorship of a work, determines the applicable legal framework, documents the consent for the use of personal data, and verifies the authenticity of digital artifacts.

The goal is to integrate the front-end and back-end components into a usable, deployable service. The project is carried out by the Department of Legal Sciences and the Department of Economics and Statistics of the University of Udine.

In this project I work on the design and implementation of the protocols, applications and interactions with the blockchain platform.

Funded under PR FESR FVG 2021-2027, bando a1.3.1 "Strumento di incentivazione a fondo perduto per la realizzazione di Proof of Concept (PoC)". More details on the [project's web page](https://www.uniud.it/it/ricerca/progetti-e-iniziative/progetti-finanziati/pr-fesr/truthster-poc-implementazione-della-piattaforma-truthster-per-certificazione-di-media-digitali-mediante-blockchain).
