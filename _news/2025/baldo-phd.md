title: Welcome to Massimiliano Baldo, new PhD student!
---
We are delighted to announce that [Massimiliano Baldo](https://baldomassimiliano.com/) has joined our research group as student of the [National PhD Program in Cybersecurity](https://cysec.imtlucca.it/).

Massimiliano's research is focused on the critical intersection of modern computing and security. His interests include Programming Languages for the specification of security policies, and methods and techniques for their verification - a key issue in the area of cloud and edge computing. 

We are excited to support Massimiliano's journey and see the impact of his work on these rapidly evolving fields.
