I am honored to announce that I have been appointed as new [co-editor of the Concurrency Column](https://eatcs.org/index.php/board-of-editors) for the **Bulletin of the European Association for Theoretical Computer Science (EATCS)**, joining Nobuko Yoshida (University of Oxford) in this role.

The [Bulletin of EATCS](https://eatcs.org/index.php/eatcs-bulletin) is a cornerstone of the theoretical computer science community, providing a platform for rapid dissemination of news, ideas, and specialized columns that reflect the state of the art in our field. The **Concurrency Column** has a long-standing tradition of hosting high-quality surveys, open problems, and discussions on the foundations of concurrent and distributed systems—topics that are central to my research.

I am also happy to share that my first curated content for the column has just been released in the **February 2026 issue** (No. 148): _[Foundations of Runtime Monitoring through the Lens of Concurrency Theory](https://bulletin.eatcs.org/index.php/beatcs/article/view/876)_ by Luca Aceto et al..  
It is a privilege to begin this journey by facilitating the publication of such significant research and contributing to the dialogue within the concurrency theory community.


I look forward to contributing to the EATCS community by curating insightful content and fostering the dialogue between researchers in concurrency theory.
